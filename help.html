We're here to support your transition to life in Oregon

Comprehensive Support Services

At Peace Action Network, we provide a range of services to help refugees settle into their new communities. Below are the key areas where we can assist you and your family.

Housing Assistance

  • Temporary housing referrals
  • Rental application support
  • Tenant rights education
  • Utility setup guidance

Emergency Resources

24/7 Crisis Support

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help:

  • Emergency: Dial 911
  • Crisis Line: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Domestic Violence: 1-800-799-SAFE (7233)

Basic Needs

For immediate food, shelter, or clothing:

  • Portland Rescue Mission: (503) 906-7690
  • Oregon Food Bank: (503) 282-0555
  • Transition Projects: (503) 280-4700
