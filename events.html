Join us in creating welcoming spaces for refugees in our community

Tutoring Refugee Kids

July 15, 2023 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM Community Center, Portland

Our weekly tutoring program provides academic support to refugee children who are adjusting to new schools and often learning English as a second language. Volunteers work one-on-one or in small groups to help with homework, reading, and basic math skills.

This program is especially important as many refugee parents are still learning English themselves and may struggle to help with schoolwork. We provide all necessary materials and training for volunteers.

What to Expect:

  • Orientation for new volunteers at 2:30 PM
  • Snacks provided for all participants
  • Translation support available for several languages
  • Opportunity to build meaningful relationships

Summer Picnic for Seniors

August 5, 2023 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM Washington Park, Portland

Our annual summer picnic brings together refugee seniors and community members for a day of food, games, and companionship. Many refugee seniors experience isolation after resettlement, and this event provides a welcoming space to connect with others.

We'll have traditional foods from several cultures, live music, and activities suitable for all mobility levels. Volunteers are needed to help with setup, serving food, facilitating activities, and most importantly, engaging in conversation with participants.

Event Highlights:

  • Traditional music and dance performances
  • Cultural food tasting from around the world
  • Gentle outdoor games and activities
  • Shaded seating areas and wheelchair accessibility

Cultural Exchange Dinner

September 10, 2023 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM Peace Action Network Headquarters

Join us for an evening of food and storytelling as we celebrate the diverse cultures represented in our refugee community. This potluck-style dinner encourages participants to share a dish from their culture and the story behind it.

The event fosters cross-cultural understanding and creates opportunities for refugees to share their heritage with pride. We'll have a short program featuring refugee speakers sharing their resettlement experiences.

Participant Information:

  • Please bring a dish to share (optional)
  • Recipe cards will be available to exchange
  • Children welcome with supervised activities
  • Transportation assistance available upon request
