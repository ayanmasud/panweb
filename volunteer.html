Volunteer With Us

Join our team and make a difference in refugees' lives

Become Part of Our Community

Volunteers are the heart of Peace Action Network. Whether you can give a few hours a week or want to take on a leadership role, we have opportunities that match your skills and interests.

Tutoring & Education

Help refugee children and adults with English language skills, homework help, and cultural orientation.

  • ESL tutors
  • Homework helpers
  • Computer skills instructors

Direct Support

Work directly with refugee families to help them adjust to life in Oregon.

  • Family mentors
  • Transportation assistance
  • Cultural orientation guides

Event Support

Help with our community events and cultural celebrations.

  • Event planning
  • Set-up/clean-up crews
  • Activity facilitators

Ready to Volunteer?

Send an email to pan.org.us@gmail.com with the subject "Volunteering".

Please let us know about what in specific you hope to volunteer for.

Volunteer FAQs

What is the time commitment?

Most volunteer positions require a minimum commitment of 2-4 hours per week for at least 3 months. Some one-time or event-based opportunities are also available.



Do I need special qualifications?

Most positions don't require special qualifications, though some (like ESL tutoring) may require training we provide. The most important qualification is a desire to help!
